Welcome to Parity!


Parity first started off as a small chatroom for school so we could collaborate, ask questions to each other, and chill and talk.


And the funny thing is, it turned out to be a disaster.


People were getting chaotic, the hosting service accused me of stuff, and things were pretty much scrambled just like scrambled eggs :/.


But in the end, it was for a learning experience and for fun. In the beginning, it was only me (a middle schooler), who almost built it entirely on PHP.


And little did I know it would turn out to be much more than being just a chatroom for school.


Later on, Parity improved more, I got more devs, and in the end, I got more knowledge and, overall, it was very fun to make!


And when I say we improved it, I REALLY MEAN WE IMPROVED IT!


Seriously, it may seem really simple right now to a normal person, but we started with a single page and JavaScript echoing out the contents of a text box whenever you clicked enter, and then you could only see messages on one screen.


But now here we are: Parity, a rip-off off version of Discord.


So here is how Parity works.


There are 4 chat rooms: General, Programming, Off Topic, Work, and Potato (which is only for people who are on potatoes, if you know what I mean, lol).


Right now, we are currently developing other stuff in Parity, such as more rooms (not only chat rooms though), better user experience, etc.


So if you have any questions, suggestions, requests, and possibly also even want to become a dev, then feel free to email me!
