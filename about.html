Welcome to Parity!


Parity first started off as a small chatroom for school during COVID times so we could collaborate during virtual school
(some of our teachers were terrible) and chill.


The funny thing is, it turned out to be a disaster because the dev was literal dog water.


Things were getting chaotic, the hosting service accused me of exploiting their servers, and things were scrambled harder than that time I dropped my omlette :/.


But in the end, it was a great learning experience and quite fun. In the beginning, it was only me (a middle schooler), who built the entire thing on PHP.


Little did I know it would turn out to be much more than being just a tiny school chatroom.


Later on, Parity improved more and more, I got more devs, and in the end, I learned a lot, and overall, it was a lot of fun to create!


And when I say we improved it, I REALLY MEAN WE IMPROVED IT!


Seriously, it may seem really simple right now to a normal person, but we started with a single page and JavaScript echoing out the contents of a text box whenever you clicked enter so that you could only see messages on one screen.


But well here we are now: Parity, a Walmart version of Discord.


So here's how Parity works:


There are 5 chatrooms: General, Programming, Off Topic, Work, and Potato
(which is only for people who are on potatoes, if you know what I mean, lol).


Right now, we are currently developing other stuff in Parity, such as more rooms (not only text chat *wink*), better user experience, etc.


So if you have any questions, suggestions, requests, and possibly also even want to become a dev, then feel free to email me!


P.S. We have apps
